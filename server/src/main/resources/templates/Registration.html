Terms and Conditions

1. Reservation Policy

By making a reservation at Romantic Baboy Korean Grill, you agree to arrive on time for your scheduled appointment. Please note that your table will be held for 15 minutes past your reserved time.

2. Cancellation Policy

Reservations can be cancelled for free as long as your table status is still "Waiting/Pending". Once your table is ready and the status changes to "Ready" or "Seated", cancellations will no longer be allowed. Please arrive on time to avoid missing your reservation.

3. No-Show Policy

Failure to arrive for your reservation without prior cancellation will be marked as a no-show. Repeated no-shows may result in restricted booking privileges.

4. Guest Count

The number of guests indicated in your reservation should be accurate. Significant changes (more than 2 persons) may affect table availability.

5. Walk-In Policy

Walk-in customers are welcome, but reservations are prioritized. Waiting times may vary during peak hours.

6. Privacy

Your personal information will only be used for reservation purposes and will not be shared with third parties without your consent.

7. Dietary Restrictions

Please inform our staff of any allergies or dietary restrictions upon arrival. While we accommodate various dietary needs, cross-contamination cannot be guaranteed.

8. Payment

Payment is to be settled directly at the restaurant. We accept cash and select credit cards.
